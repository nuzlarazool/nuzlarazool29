At Rina Bake House, we believe in the magic of baking and the joy it brings to life. Our journey began with a simple love for creating delicious, handcrafted treats that delight the senses and warm the heart. From our kitchen to your table, we take pride in using only the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques to ensure that each product is a masterpiece of flavor and quality.

Founded by Abdul Razool, a passionate baker with 18 years of experience, Rina Bake House is more than just a bakery; it’s a celebration of the art of baking. Whether you're craving a classic loaf of sourdough, a decadent chocolate cake, or a flaky pastry, our menu features a delightful array of sweet and savory options, all made with love and attention to detail

We’re committed to creating not just exceptional baked goods, but memorable experiences. At Rina Bake House, every visit is an opportunity to indulge in the comfort and joy of freshly baked goodness, and we look forward to sharing that experience with you.

Thank you for choosing us. We can’t wait to welcome you and treat you to something truly special
